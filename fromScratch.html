About Kobe Bryant

Kobe Dai Bryant was an American professional basketball player who spent his entire 20-season career with the Los Angeles Lakers. He is widely considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Born on August 23, 1978, Kobe passed away on January 26, 2020.

He inspired the world with his work ethic, scoring ability, and relentless drive. The Kobe Bryant story is also one of reinvention, from athlete to storyteller.
